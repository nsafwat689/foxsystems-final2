# Fox Systems SEO Upgrade Strategy

## 1. Brand Keyword Optimization
*   **Target Variations:** Fox Systems, Fox Systems Tech, FoxSystem, FoxSystems, Fox Systems Egypt.
*   **Strategy:** Ensure all variations are included in the meta keywords and scattered naturally in the content (especially in the footer and about section).

*   **Cybersecurity:** "Cybersecurity Services Egypt", "Network Security Solutions Cairo", "Firewall Protection Egypt".
*   **Infrastructure:** "IT Infrastructure Setup Egypt", "Data Center Solutions Cairo", "Network Cabling Services Egypt".

*   **Web Development:** "Corporate Website Development Egypt", "UI/UX Design Cairo", "SEO Optimized Websites Egypt".

## 3. Technical SEO Improvements
*   **Dynamic Metadata:** Ensure every page has a unique, keyword-rich title and description.
*   **Breadcrumb Schema:** Implement for all pages to improve SERP appearance.
*   **Sitemap Optimization:** Update `sitemap.xml` to include all language variations and prioritize key pages.
*   **Robots.txt:** Ensure it correctly points to the sitemap and doesn't block essential resources.
